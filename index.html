Organiser's Message

Dear Colleague,

The Department of Civil Engineering at EPOKA University is pleased to invite you to the 5th International Balkans Conference on Challenges of Civil Engineering (BCCCE 2026), which will take place in Tirana, Albania, on 29–30 October 2026.

The BCCCE conference series has become an established platform uniting researchers, academics, and professionals to discuss recent developments and challenges in civil engineering. The conference promotes scientific exchange, collaboration, and the dissemination of innovative research across various crucial areas of the discipline.

We look forward to your participation in this excellent forum and warmly invite you to join us in Tirana, a vibrant and enjoyable city to visit.

Sincerely,
The 5th BCCCE 2026 Organising Committee
