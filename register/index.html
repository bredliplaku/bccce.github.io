Initial Registration

The intended participants will be registered when they have submitted their abstracts/proposals, papers, panels, workshops, or media productions. All fees are in Euro (€).

Accompanying Local Person (without proceedings) 100 € 150 €
International Student * 150 € 200 €

Additional Paper 100 € 100 €
Student (without proceedings) * shall be charged with an additional 50 € (certificate of participation + coffee breaks).
* Students shall provide a certificate confirming they are in a Master or PhD programme (in the academic year of 2025/2026) in order to be eligible for the reduced fee.
** Local participants are considered all the citizens of Albania and Kosovo.
NOTE: Registration fee is valid for one paper only. If you wish to submit another paper, another registration should be made.
NOTE: Participants shall make the registration no later than 30 September 2026.
