The technical program for BCCCE 2026 is architected to confront the multi-dimensional complexities of the modern built environment. Our selected technical tracks represent a rigorous fusion of traditional structural mastery and the disruptive digital frontiers of civil engineering. We invite global experts to present original work that scales from the atomic level of construction materials to the macro-dynamics of urban seismic resilience.
