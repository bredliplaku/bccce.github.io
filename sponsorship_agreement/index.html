Sponsorship Agreement

Partner with BCCCE 2026 and connect your brand with the premier civil engineering gathering in the Balkans.

Why Sponsor? Why Exhibit?

National & International Audience

BCCCE attracts delegates from across Albania and from more than 20 countries around the world. Here is your opportunity to see and be seen by a truly national audience, and to present your company to a motivated international audience.

Educated & Experienced Attendees

BCCCE delegates are university educated. Many have undertaken post-graduate studies and possess post-graduate degrees. Delegates are experienced professionals, working in their respective fields for many years.

Designers & Decision Makers

BCCCE delegates are engineers who design and specify for a wide range of civil works in Albania and Balkan countries. They also manage those who design, specify and purchase for a wide variety of projects, applications, and clients.

Targeted Promotion Opportunity

BCCCE is the premiere gathering of the civil engineering profession in Albania. Put your firm in front of those who make decisions. By sponsoring the 2026 BCCCE in Tirana, you align yourself with a major event, showcasing your firm to our delegates.
